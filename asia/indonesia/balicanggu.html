Went to Bali.

Hung out with cousin Patricia. Lucky enough to stay with her in a villa she arranged with her work.

Had a night out at some pool-turned-skate-park bar, and then a Mexican bar for a tick tock.

Made our way to a crazy dance night. We got down like we hadn't in a while.

Drove back on a scooter, through the rural parts. Looked up and saw a sky full of stars. I had not seen a starry sky like that in a long time.

Bali is a party and touristy place now. I wonder how much of the BS you have to sift through to really see local culture. How far must you tread ast the party towns of Kuta, the tailored streets of Semenyak, the Hipster parts of Canggu and the temples of Ubud. I want to go back to discover more of the nature of this place. The people on the ground. Bali, pre E-P-L.

Will be back-
