Why "SIN.GAY.PORE"? It's a jab at "homosexuality being a sin" according to the penal code in Singapore, but also a recognition that there is a gay scene here, despite conceptualizations of rigidity and oppression of the local government. So here's "Singaypore", a small photo diary into the celebration of gender, expression, and general debauchery amongst locals and foreigners who have clustered on the Little Red Dot.
