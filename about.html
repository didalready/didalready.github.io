ABOUT

THIS is a world, not big and scary, but one where humans have lived between times of War and Peace, striving to be gods while only mere mortals.

THIS is a recollection of moments; the feelings of landscapes that have transformed and shaped human civilizations; the taste of foods that have sustained cultures for centuries.

THIS is a response to my parents, who always ask me, "Where have you been lately?", to which I say:

THIS is where I've been already.
