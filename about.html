Dear User,

THIS blog is written in HTML and CSS, and pushed onto the W W W by GitHub. We've kept it as open source as possible; no fancy effects, paying homage to the 2002 era of website design: simple, and functional.

THIS is what we realized: the world isn't a big scary place. It's a place where humans have lived between War and Peace, and have continued doing so with gusto and spirit. They've lived and striven to be gods, only to find out they're mere mortals.

THIS diary allows us to preserve then recall special moments on our trip; describe then feel landscapes that transforms and shapes human civilization; savor then retaste food that has sustained cultures for centuries.

THIS is our generic endeavor. We saw and heard some things, happened to have a camera and laptop, and decided to share it. We happened to have some fun along the way, too.
