Dear User,

THIS blog is written in HTML and CSS, and deployed by BitBalloon. We don't believe in having to pay for a domain; We don't believe modern websites must be designed with fancy effects. We pay homage to the 2002 era of website design: a simpler and functional time, when people took themselves less seriously.

THIS is a privileged endeavor -- having the time, money, leisure, ability -- to travel, and acknowledging what has allowed us to be who we are, where we are, when we want. 

THIS is what we realized: the world is not a big scary place. Rather, it's a place where humans have lived among War and Peace, and have continued to do so with gusto and spirit; to have lived and striven to be gods, when they are mere mortals.

THIS is a way for us to preserve, then recall a moment in time; describe, then feel the landscape that transforms and shapes human civilization; savor, then retaste the food that has sustained cultures for centuries.

THIS is our humble, generic endeavor. A blog of all our memories.
